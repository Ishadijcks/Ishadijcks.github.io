Changelog


0.5 - Elite Four!

Release Date: Jul. 24, 2016

  • Introduction

    • Hey everybody, the new update is finally live, sorry it took so long. As with any update I'd highly recommend to reset your savefile, so you can experience the game as I intended.
      I've made a subreddit where you can report any bug you might find.
      Enjoy the new update :)

      Cheers!

  • Elite Four

    • Do you think you are strong enough to defeath the 4 masters of the Indigo League?
      Take on the Elite Four to prove your ultimate Pokemon Trainer skills!

  • Oak Items

    • Professor Oak noticed you are struggling with your journey to become the very best.
      He decided to help out by rewarding you with special items.
      Unlock these items by catching Pokemon!

  • Shinies

    • Shiny Pokemon have been spotted in the Kanto region!
      One in every 8192 Pokemon is shiny, so catching them gives you the ultimate bragging rights.
      You can double your shiny encounter chance by using one of Oaks Items.

  • Small changes

    • Professor Oak now gives a small tutorial.
    • Pokemon now stop getting exp if you don't have enough badges.
    • Added Pokemon attack upgrades.
    • Buffed Click multiplier upgrades.
    • Completely changed the exp and money formulas.
    • Pokedex now shows shinies caught instead of Pokemon bred.

  • Bug fixes

    • Fixed a bug where you could catch the Pokemon of a gym leader.
    • Money now updates correctly after buying an upgrade.
    • Fixed a faulty console log.
    • Pokeballs now show correctly.

0.4 - Badges

Release Date: Jun. 28, 2016

  • Gyms

    • You can now challenge all of the gym leaders in the Indigo League!
    Gym leaders are timed battles, defeat all their pokemon in 30 seconds to earn your badge.
    Replay gym battles for 10% of their original reward.

    Good luck!

  • Towns

    • Most towns are added to the Kanto map.
    Clear all the routes to a town to get access to it
    You can fight gym leaders in towns.

  • Notifications

    • Instead of a boring console, interesting messages are now displayed in the top right of the screen.
    Catching pokemon, evolving pokemon and buying upgrades all get a notification.
    Enjoy!

  • Bug fixes

  • Fixed catchtime being able to go into the negative.
  • Made the pokeball transparant so it doesn't overlap with other info.
  • Changed the intro text to correspond with recent updates.
  • All legendary birds now have the same spawn chance.
  • Added % sign to the catch rate.
  • Colors of completed routes now update after the right amount of kills.

0.3 - Pokédex

Release Date: Jun. 26, 2016

  • Pokédex

    • The pokédex is here! See how many of each pokémon you have defeated and captured!
    Get up to 60% bonus exp by killing the same pokémon over and over. Defeat pokémon to unlock their dex entry.

  • Small changes

  • Decreased base catch rate
  • Increased catch rate upgrades
  • Changed base catch time to 3 second (old value: 3 seconds)
  • Rebalanced some catchtime upgrades
  • Changelog doesn't automatically open when you start the game anymore

  • Bug fixes

  • Removed a few faulty prints
  • Changed 'e' to 'é'
  • Removed the map button from the navbar at the bottom of the screen

0.09 - Legendaries

Release Date: Sep. 18, 2015

Legendaries

  • The player now has a small chance to encounter legendaries
    • Legendaries are rare pokémon who are a lot stronger than other pokémon
    • Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres, Mew and Mewtwo are all in the game!
    • Everytime a pokémon spawns it has a chance to be a legendary
    • This chance can of course be improved by buying upgrades

0.08 - Dynamic catch rate

Release Date: Sep. 16, 2015

pokémon

  • Catch rate is no longer fixed for all pokémon
    • Weak pokémon have a higher chance to be caught than strong pokémon
    • Your overall catch chance can still be upgraded!

    Savefile

  • You no longer need to delete your savefile every time the game updates

0.07 - Evolution

Release Date: Sep. 15, 2015

pokémon

  • pokémon can now evolve!
    • When a pokémon reaches a high enough level it can evolve
    • When a pokémon evolves, you keep the pokémon and get the evolution at level 1
    • Example: I have a level 15 Pidgey, and it reaches level 16. I end up with a level 16 pidgey and a level 1 pidgeotto
