0.7 - Shops, Quests, Chat and a lot more!

Release Date: Oct. 5, 2016

  • Introduction

    • Heyo, shops and quests are finally here!
      I think quests are pretty fun :)
      Thanks everyone for helping out!

  • Chat

  • PokeClicker now has a chat!
  • Join here
  • I am in this chat almost 24/7, so it's the perfect way to contact me!

  • Shops

  • Use your well earned money and shard to buy items in various cities.
  • From evolution stones to X Exp, all items can be bought.
  • Buying lots of items in a row increases their price.
  • Don't worry, defeat Pokemon to decrease the prices again!

  • Random Quests

  • Take on endless randomized challenges to earn Quest Points!
  • Use your Quest Points in the shops throughout Kanto.
  • Quests become harder as you complete more of them on a single day.
  • Skip prices and difficulty reset daily.

  • Bug fixes

  • Removed a faulty console log.
  • Updated indicator for caught shinies.
  • Updated the shiny charm image.
  • Fixed Pidgey showing up twice on route 8.
  • Fixed Charmeleon and Wartortle evolution levels.
  • Click and Pokemon attack can no longer drop below 1.
  • Fixed not getting tokens for recapturing shinies.

0.6 - Dungeons, Items and Shards!

Release Date: Sep. 9, 2016

  • Introduction

    • Heyo, this is a big update!
      I've added dungeons, types, shards and items!
      I'm sorry it took so long, I've been busy with real live stuff. But more updates will arive soon, I already have some great ideas :)
      It is highly highly recommended to reset all you progress while playing this update. It will make the game much more fun!

  • Upgrades

    • Upgrades got nerfed a lot.
    • All previously purchased upgrades are reverted.

  • Dungeons

    • Dou you think you have what it takes to explore all the dungeons in the Kanto region?
    • Explore all the rooms to battle dungeon exclusive pokemon, and gain rare items!
    • Each dungeon also has a boss, so watch out!
    • Enter a dungeon using dungeon tokens. They are quite rare so spend them wisely!

  • Types and Shards

    • PokeClicker now supports types. The damage your Pokemon do now depends on their type and the type of your enemy.
    • Gain 1 type Shard for every pokemon you defeat and upgrade your type effectiveness in the shards menu.
    • You can use Shards to buy evolution stones!

  • Items

    • Some Pokemon will drop rare items.
    • You can use these items to boost your attack, exp, money and a lot more!
    • Items are only active for 60 seconds, so use them wisely!

  • Small changes

    • Some html elements can no longer be selected.
    • Added an indication of how many Oak items you are using.

  • Big changes

    • Exp formula completely reworked.
    • Money formula completely reworked.
    • Increased enemy health.

  • Bug fixes

    • Fixed a bug where pokemon could level over their cap.
    • Fixed pokemon being at level 0.
    • Fixed exp and money going to NaN.
    • Fuchsia city no longer uses Pewter city's image.
